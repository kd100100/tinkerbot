Your Task for Round 1 is to make a car move in a square using Arduino and 4 DC motors. The car should complete only 1 lap of the square and stop. Judging will be primarily based on the time taken to complete the task. The maximum time allotted is 30 minutes. Even if you fail to complete the task, marks will be awarded based on your partially completed circuit and code. You will be given 10 mins to read through these instructions and to ask doubts if you have any. Participants should share screen throughout the round, failing to do will result in direct disqualification. After completing the task double check your output before showing the results to the coordinators.

Marking Scheme:
- Code - 5 marks
- Circuit - 5 marks
- Simulation - 5 marks

If you complete your task and show the output within time, full marks will be awarded for code, circuit, and simulation. Further, the winner will be judged based on the time taken.
If you face any technical issues in connecting to the meet, contact the coordinators through the WhatsApp group.

Hints to complete your task faster than others:
-It is not mandatory to use a motor driver. You can use the Arduino GPIO instead to control the DC motor.
-You can write your entire code in the setup function itself as the car has to complete only 1 lap.
